How do mneumonic seeds word? (12 or 24 seed words)

Youtube Explanation

Bitcoin Improvement Proposal (BIP) 39 defines mneumonic seed words.

There are 2048 mneumonic seed words.

The seed words used for your recovery are input into a cryptographic hash along with a user-defined passphrase.

This hash is then used to generate a private key, generally using BIP32 methodology.

Note: Don't select your own words, use hardware/software designed for the purpose.
